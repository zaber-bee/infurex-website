## 🎨 Brand Voice Guidelines

### Tone of Voice
- **Professional** yet approachable
- **Confident** without being arrogant
- **Results-focused** (emphasize outcomes, not just features)
- **Aspirational** (premium, luxury, excellence)

### Do's
✅ Use action verbs: "Dominate," "Transform," "Elevate," "Convert"  
✅ Be specific: "425% lead increase" vs "more leads"  
✅ Address pain points: "outdated website," "lost opportunities"  
✅ Show empathy: "We understand..."

### Don'ts
❌ Avoid jargon: "synergistic solutions," "paradigm shift"  
❌ Don't be pushy: "Buy now!" or "Limited time!"  
❌ Avoid clichés: "think outside the box," "game-changer"  
❌ Don't over-promise: "Guaranteed #1 ranking"
